The end-to-end property management software

Features for Landlords

Tenant tracking

Features of tenant tracking:

  • Track rent and deposits due from Tenants
  • Send invoices to Tenants for payments due
  • Receive online payments from Tenants
  • Charge tenants late fees automatically
  • Support all types of lease terms: monthly, weekly, paid in arrears, etc...

Applicant screening

Features of applicant screening:

  • Create custom application forms
  • Allow Applicants to submit application forms online
  • Run background checks on Applicants
  • Easily convert Applicants into Tenants

Online Payments

Features of online payments:

  • Configure the bank account where you want to receive online payments
  • Tenants can send one-time online payments to you
  • Tenants can set up recurring online payments to be sent to you on a schedule
  • View the status of online payments on the main and tenant dashboards

Accounting

Features of accounting:

  • Add your rental-related income and expense transactions
  • Configure recurring transactions to be generated on a schedule
  • Import transactions automatically from your bank and credit card accounts
  • Reconcile your bank and credit card statements against the transactions logged in Housters
  • Fully customize the types that your transactions get categorized as
  • Switch to double-entry mode and input all debit and credit lines for each transaction
  • Track rental-related trips and then deduct them on your taxes

Task Management

Features of task management:

  • Manage rental-related to-do list tasks
  • Organize tasks into separate custom lists or by property or tenant
  • Accept maintenance requests from tenants, which automatically get added to your to-do lists
  • Share tasks with contractors
  • Receive invoices from contractors
  • Send online payments to contractors

Reporting

Features of reporting:

  • View important financial figures such as net operating income and cash on cash invested on your main and property dashboards
  • Run our accounting and tax reports to get summaries for certain properties and date ranges
  • Customize the branding on your reports so that they have your logo and business name

Features for Tenants

Online payments

Features of online payment:

  • View your rent status on the tenant portal's main dashboard
  • View any open rent or deposit invoices due to the landlord or property manager
  • Configure the bank account where you want to send online payments from
  • Send one-time online payments from the tenant portal to your landlord or property manager
  • Set up recurring online payments to be sent to your landlord or property manager on a schedule
  • View the status of online payments on the tenant portal's main dashboard

Maintenance Requests

Features of maintenance requests:

  • Submit maintenance requests from the tenant portal to your landlord or property manager
  • View the status of previously submitted maintenance requests on the tenant portal
  • Submitted maintenance requests will be automatically added to your landlord or property manager's to-do list

Features for Contractors

Shared To-Do Lists

Features of shared to-do lists:

  • Invite your client landlords and property managers to Housters from the contractor portal
  • View to-do list tasks assigned to you by your landlord and property manager clients on the contractor portal
  • Update the statuses of to-do list tasks, as you perform the work
  • Landlords and property managers will see the updated progress in their own to-do lists

Invoicing

Features of invoicing:

  • Generate invoices for the work that was completed on the contractor portal
  • Invoices can be exported to PDF and then sent to your landlord and property manager clients

Online payments

Features of online payment:

  • Configure the bank account where you would like to receive online payments from the contractor portal
  • Your landlord and property manager clients can send you online payments for the work you performed
  • View the status of online payments on the main dashboard of the contractor portal
